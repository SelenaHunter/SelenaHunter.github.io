About

Growth and comfort do not coexist.

I've been an artist and designer for years, but I like the challenge of building. I like JavaScript, a lot. (It reminds me of happy days spent programming Flash games in ActionScript, okay?) I also like React, a lot. (Don't judge me.) I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow and become a better developer.

Strong Opinions, Loosely Held.

I'm a firm believer in cooperating with and understanding your fellow human beings. My goal is to make the world a more accepting place, one page at a time. The internet needs more accessibility, including support for people with disabilities, as well as gender-neutral language for people who might not fit into typical boxes.

Curiosity, confidence, courage, and constancy.

I'm proud to be a queer female developer. My pronouns are she/her, but they/theirs or even he/his is fine too. Whatever's easiest.

I like video games more than TV or movies. If the TV is on, it'll be the Disney Channel. I listen to ridiculous playlists made for my favorite characters while I'm working. If I'm not coding or learning, I'm probably drawing and writing with my SO and furry, four-legged baby.
